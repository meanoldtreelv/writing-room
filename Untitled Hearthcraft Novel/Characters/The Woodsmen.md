# Village Families — The Woodsmen

## Gareth — Woodcutter/Carpenter
- **Age:** 50s
- **Role:** Maintains the village's structures — repairs, builds, shapes. The reason the houses fit so perfectly between the roots.
- **Gift:** Minor — can sense wood quality, feel where a tree wants to be cut. Not circle-casting, just intuition.
- **Personality:** Massive, gentle, talks to trees. The village thinks this is eccentric. He thinks it's polite. "you wouldn't just grab someone's arm without asking."
- **Relationship with Brin:** Kindred spirits. They both listen to the forest. He told her, when she was small, "Grandmother likes your singing." She thought he meant her actual grandmother. Maybe he did. Maybe both.
- **His gift:** Listening to where the wood wants to be. The village's houses fit between the roots because Gareth asked the roots where they'd allow walls.

## Brenna — Gareth's Wife
- **Age:** 40s
- **Role:** Manages the village's wood supply and is the village's best weaver — bark cloth, plant fiber, whatever the forest provides.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Sharp, practical.
- **Made Brin's traveling clothes:** Good boots, warm cloak, sewing kit.
- **Departure:** "you'll need these more than you need magic."

## Lief — Their Son
- **Age:** 16
- **Role:** Apprentice to his father.
- **Gift:** None. But good hands.
- **Personality:** Quiet, steady, follows his father's example.

## Fern — Their Daughter
- **Age:** 13
- **Role:** Brin's closest friend.
- **Gift:** None. Doesn't want one.
- **Personality:** Wiry, quick, loud. Thinks Brin's magic is "fine" but she's more interested in climbing trees, catching frogs, and racing the other kids through the Outer Wood.
- **Relationship with Brin:** Best friends — not because they're alike, but because they're not. Fern is everything Brin isn't: loud, impulsive, unafraid of looking foolish. Brin grounds her. Fern lifts her.
- **Departure:** Fern doesn't cry. She gives Brin a smooth stone from the river — "so you don't forget what mud looks like." Then she climbs the nearest tree and watches from above as Brin walks down the road. She stays up there until the village can't see Brin anymore.
- **Potential:** Fern might be the next one with the gift. Or she might not. Either way, she's the village Brin is fighting for.
