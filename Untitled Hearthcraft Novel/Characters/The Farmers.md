# Village Families — The Farmers

## Thorn — Lead Farmer
- **Age:** 50s
- **Role:** Manages the garden circles — not the magic, the *planning.* What goes where, when to plant, when to rotate.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Weathered, stubborn, argues with the soil. Grumpy, particular, absolutely essential. He's been farming these shade beds for thirty years and he knows every one by name (he names them. "old grumpy" is the bed that never cooperates).
- **Relationship with Brin:** Professional respect. He doesn't understand her magic but he understands results. "your circles work. my plans work. together we eat. that's enough."

## Willa — Thorn's Wife
- **Age:** 50s
- **Role:** Does the hardest physical labor in the village — turning soil, hauling water, maintaining irrigation. The village's midwife. She delivered every child in the village for twenty years, including Brin.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Strong, silent, the village's backbone. She's stronger than half the men and doesn't care who knows it.
- **Departure:** Squeezes Brin's shoulder once, hard, and walks away.

## Ash — Their Son
- **Age:** 19
- **Role:** Works the farm.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Has a quiet crush on Fenn** that everyone except Fenn has noticed.

## Briar — Their Daughter
- **Age:** 15
- **Role:** Helps her mother.
- **Gift:** None. Strong like Willa.
