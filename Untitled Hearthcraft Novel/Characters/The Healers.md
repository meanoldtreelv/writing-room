# Village Families — The Healers

## Corwin — Herbalist/Healer
- **Age:** 50s
- **Role:** Village healer. Manages the herb garden, the drying shed, the medicine stores.
- **Gift:** Minor — can feel injuries, sense illness, but can't heal with circles. His talent is diagnostic, not restorative. He knows what's wrong but can't fix it with magic. This has made him *excellent* with herbs and poultices.
- **Personality:** Gaunt, precise, irritable. He was Maven's student for years. She tried to teach him the songs but he couldn't hear them. He's never forgiven this — not Maven, himself. He compensates by being the best non-magical healer he can.
- **Relationship with Brin:** Complicated. He respects her gift and resents it. She can do what he trained his whole life to do, effortlessly. But he's also the one who taught her which herbs work, which poultices draw infection, which roots ease pain. Her magic is stronger because of his knowledge.
- **Departure:** He gives her a satchel of dried herbs and a hand-written guide. "in case you forget." She won't. But she takes it.

## Hesta — Corwin's Wife
- **Age:** 40s
- **Role:** Makes Corwin tolerable. Translates his irritability into something the village can work with. "He's not angry, he's worried. Same face, different reason."
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Calm, steady, the bridge.
- **Helps with:** Herb garden, drying shed management.
- **Departure scene:** Tells Brin the morning she leaves, "he'll miss you. he won't say it. but he'll miss you." Talking about Corwin. Also talking about herself.

## Pax — Their Son
- **Age:** 12
- **Gift:** None. But fascinated by Brin's. Follows her around and asks questions. "does it hurt? does it tickle? can you do it to me?" She circles him once, playfully, and he says he feels warm for the rest of the day.
- **Role:** Child. Future village member. Potential carrier of the gift? Too early to tell.
