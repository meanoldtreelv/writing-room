# Village Families — The Keeper

## Pell — Keeper of Stores/Granary
- **Age:** 60s
- **Role:** Manages the village's shared stores — grain, dried food, preserved goods, seeds. Knows exactly how much the village has, how much it needs, and how long it will last.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Thin, meticulous, counts everything. Paranoid about stores in the way that only someone who's seen a bad winter can be. Every seed is counted. Every grain is weighed. He trusts the numbers.
- **Relationship with Brin:** He trusts her circles more than he trusts his counts. "you keep the stores warm, I'll keep them counted. between us, nobody starves." When she leaves, his hands shake while he checks the granary. The stores are full. They'll hold. But he checks again anyway.
- **Knows about the night:** He was a child. He remembers the screaming. He remembers his mother holding him. He counts things because counting is controllable and the night was not.

## Mira — Pell's Wife
- **Age:** 50s
- **Role:** Helps Pell with the stores. Manages the seed bank — next year's planting, carefully preserved.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Gentle, worried, the village's conscience. She worries most openly. About winter, about the stores, about the children, about Brin leaving.
- **Departure:** Brings Brin a packet of seeds "from home. in case you want to grow something there."
- **Surrogate grandmother:** Since Maven died, Mira has been the one who braids Brin's hair sometimes and tells her stories about Maven that Brin hasn't heard.

**Their children:** Grown and married into other families.
