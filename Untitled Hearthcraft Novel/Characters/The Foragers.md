# Village Families — The Foragers

## Moss — Forager/Mushroom Keeper
- **Age:** 40s
- **Role:** Knows the forest floor better than anyone. Every mushroom, every root, every edible thing that grows in the deep shade. Provides the village's trade goods — the rare mushrooms the merchant values.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Quiet, observant, disappears into the forest for days. The village jokes that Moss uses his yearly word budget on the merchant.
- **Relationship with Brin:** He taught her which mushrooms are safe and which ones "sing" (her word — some fungi have a faint resonance that only gifted ears can hear). He never commented on the "singing." He just pointed and said "those ones."

## Dara — Moss's Wife
- **Age:** 30s
- **Role:** The village's gossip, news-keeper, and social glue. Runs the drying racks for mushrooms and herbs — the village's processing center.
- **Gift:** None.
- **Personality:** Warm, chatty. Compensates for her husband's silence.
- **Departure:** Packed Brin's bag with dried mushrooms "for the road" and whispered "you come back safe, you hear me? we need you more than they do."

## Rowan — Their Daughter
- **Age:** 10
- **Gift:** None. Too young for much.
- **Role:** Child.

## Sage — Their Son
- **Age:** 7
- **Gift:** None. Scared of everything.
- **Best friend:** Pax (Corwin's son).
