The Loomworks (shitty title, i know) is a man-out-of-time story. Emily is thrown forward 100 years and gets to deal with it as best she can. This story was mainly started because i wanted to write about Callum again. He and his sister were the protagonists of some short stories i wrote years ago, that ended with his sister going off to college in the UK. Those stories dont have much bearing on this one, as the narratives are disconnected. The only real carry over is that Callum isn't going to be one short for a fight. An angry Englishman has nothing on a full grown grizzly bear.
