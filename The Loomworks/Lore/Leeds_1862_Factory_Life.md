# Leeds_1862_Factory_Life

**Setting**: Leeds, England, 1862. Characterized by:
*   **Industrialization**: Dominated by mills and factories, especially on the riverfront. The intense, relentless pace of factory work is central.
*   **Environment**: Stifling heat, lint-filled air, pervasive grime, and pollution (river).
*   **Labor Conditions**: Long working hours (14+ hours daily), physically demanding, harsh and unforgiving. Calloused hands are a commonmark of labor.
*   **Social Stratification**: A clear divide between the working class and those who benefit from their labor. The city's "pub streets" are hubs for boisterous, sometimes menacing, crowds after work.

**Emily's Context**: A young woman working long hours at the Loomworks factory, dreaming of a different life, feeling trapped by her circumstances. She has a younger brother, Tom, and a mother who worried about her hands being too delicate for work.
