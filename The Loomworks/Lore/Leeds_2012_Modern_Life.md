# Leeds_2012_Modern_Life

**Setting**: Leeds, England, Present Day (2012). Characterized by:
*   **Modern Amenities**: Sleek stone-lined riverbanks, manicured parks, personal vehicles (cars), advanced technology (smartphones).
*   **Environment**: Cleaner air (compared to 1862), more managed urban spaces. Despite modernization, echoes of industry and city life persist.
*   **Social Dynamics**: More anonymity, but also potential dangers. People are accustomed to technology and faster communication.

**Contrast with 1862**: Emily experiences profound culture shock due to the pace, technology, urban design, and social norms. The contrast highlights the progress and challenges of different eras. Callum's well-fitted, modern attire is alien to her.
