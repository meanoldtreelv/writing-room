# The Loomworks - The Mystery of Displacement

**Nature of the Phenomenon**: Currently ambiguous, with possibilities:
*   **Accidental Temporal Anomaly**: Possibly linked to the river or the historical site of the Loomworks factory, perhaps a rare confluence of environmental factors. Emily's fall into the river is the trigger.
*   **Metaphorical Loom**: The "Loomworks" could represent a force of fate or a temporal mechanism that weaves timelines. The factory's industrial processes might be a symbolic precursor to this temporal "weaving."
*   **Unseen Technology/Magic**: Less explored, but could involve an unknown device or energy source connected to the historical site.

**Discovery**: Emily's traumatic experience and subsequent appearance in 2012 are the primary evidence presented.

**Key Questions**:
*   How exactly did Emily travel through time? Was it the river, the factory site, or something she carried?
*   Why *this* specific jump (1862 to 2012)? Is there a significance to the year or the individuals involved?
*   Is the phenomenon repeatable or controllable?
*   Does the "Loomworks" have an intelligence or purpose?
