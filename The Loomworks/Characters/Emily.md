# Emily

### Overview
**Role**: The *lost thread*—a weaver from 1862 pulled into the present by the Loomworks’ *hungry* looms. She’s not a victim; she’s a *story that got misplaced*.

---
### Public Face
- **The Confused Traveler**: Overwhelmed by the modern world, but *curious*.
- **The Weaver’s Daughter**: Her hands remember the *rhythm* of the loom.
- **The Survivor**: She’s *adaptable*—because she’s always had to be.

---
### Secrets
1. **She Was Chosen**:
   - The Loomworks *pulled* her from the river because her thread was *frayed*, not broken.
   - **Why It Matters**: She was *supposed* to drown. The looms gave her a *second chance*.

2. **She Sees the Magic**:
   - Threads *glow* with memories.
   - The river *sings* to her.
   - The looms *hum* with her name.
   - **Why It Matters**: She’s not *lost*—she’s *learning* to see the world differently.

3. **Her Mother’s Thread**:
   - The looms show her her mother’s *unfinished* story.
   - **Why It Matters**: She has to decide—*weave* it back together, or *let it go*.

---
### Motivations
- **Find Her Place**: She doesn’t want to *go back*—she wants to *belong*.
- **Understand the Looms**: They *chose* her. She needs to know *why*.
- **Heal Her Thread**: Her story was *cut short*. She wants to *finish* it—on her terms.

---
### Flaws
- **Stubborn**: She *refuses* to admit she’s scared.
- **Nostalgic**: She *clings* to the past—even when it hurts.
- **Self-Sacrificing**: She’ll *weave* herself into someone else’s story before her own.

---
### Relationships
- **Callum**: The first person who *sees* her—not just her dress.
  - She *trusts* him because he *understands* lost things.
  - She *fears* he’ll *leave* her like everything else.
- **The Loomworks**: It’s not her *enemy*—it’s her *mirror*.
  - The looms *remember* her.
  - The workers *miss* her.
- **The Spider Worker**: The oldest weaver—a *spider* who *knits* instead of weaves.
  - She *knows* Emily’s mother.
  - She *offers* Emily a *choice*: *Weave* or *walk away*.

---
### Key Scenes
1. **The River’s Gift**: Waking up with *starlight* on her skin.
2. **The Glowing Spool**: The first time a *thread* chooses her.
3. **The Teacup’s Memory**: Drinking tea from 1862—and *seeing* her mother.
4. **The Final Weave**: Combining her thread with Callum’s to make a *door* to her future.

---
### Thread-Sight (Her Magic)
- **Sees Colors in Threads**:
  - *Gold*: Joy (a child’s laughter).
  - *Silver*: Sadness (a mother’s goodbye).
  - *Rainbow*: The Loomworks’ weave (the world’s story).
- **Hears the Looms**:
  - They *hum* her name.
  - They *show* her memories.
- **Understands the River**:
  - It *sings* to her at night.
  - It *chose* her for a reason.

---
**Final Note**:
*"Emily isn’t lost. She’s* **learning to weave herself into the world**—*one thread at a time."*
