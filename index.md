### A Cage Of Thorns
**Status:** active | **Genre:** dark-romantasy | **Files:** 14
**Thematic Core:** Love is a weapon in the Unseelie Court — and the one who refuses to be defined holds all the power.
**Hook:** Princess Elara is sold into marriage with Princip Dain — who is not the man she was promised. Dain's court erases gender as a power mechanism, and Elara's insistence on "him" is a deliberate provocation. Thorn magic feeds on love and draws blood.
**Key Elements:** Unseelie rules (gifts are curses, thorns listen), pronoun war as power struggle, magic with physical cost
**Entry Points:** `README.md`, `Story Hook.md`, `Story Pitch.md`

### The Loomworks
**Status:** active | **Genre:** sci-fi / man-out-of-time | **Files:** 14
**Thematic Core:** What survives when you're thrown 100 years forward — your identity, or just your stubbornness?
**Hook:** Emily, a present-day woman, is thrown to 2126. She meets Callum, a fiery Englishman with a penchant for brawling. Together they navigate a future that's wondrous and dangerous. The "Loomworks" — is it metaphor for fate or literal time-weaving tech?
**Key Elements:** Culture shock as satire, physical vs ideological conflict, adaptation vs identity
**Entry Points:** `README.md`, `Story Hook.md`, `Story Pitch.md`
