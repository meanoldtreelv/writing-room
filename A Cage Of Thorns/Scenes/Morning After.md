# The Morning After

### Setting
- **The Bridal Chamber**: The sheets are *tangled*, the thorned circlet *glints* on the pillow.
- **The Air**: Smells like *burnt sugar* and *old blood*.
- **Dain**: Already dressed, leaning against the doorframe, *watching* Elara wake up.

---
### The Scene
**Elara** (blinking, voice raw):
"You *watched* me sleep?"

**Dain** (smirking, swirling a goblet of something dark):
"Someone had to. The thorns *like* you. I wanted to make sure they didn’t *take* too much."

*(Elara’s fingers fly to her throat. The circlet is *gone*—but her skin *tingles* where it rested.)*

**Elara** (sitting up, sheets clutched to her chest):
"Where is it?"

**Dain** (tilting her head, amused):
"Where do *you* think?"

*(A *rustle* from the pillow. The circlet is *there*—but the thorns have *grown*. They’re *longer*. *Sharper*. And they’re *humming*.)

**Elara** (whispering):
"It *moved*."

**Dain** (laughing, low and dark):
"Oh, *Princess*. Thorns don’t *move*. They *wait*."

*(The circlet’s thorns *shift* again. One of them is *pointing* at Elara’s wrist.)*

**Dain** (softly, almost *gentle*):
"Put it on."

*(Elara doesn’t. The thorns *sigh*—a sound like wind through dead leaves.)*

---
### Why This Works
- **The Circlet**: It’s *alive*. It’s *hungry*. And it’s *choosing* Elara.
- **Dain’s Amusement**: She *knows* Elara is afraid. She *likes* it.
- **The Thorns**: They *react* to Elara’s *fear*. They *want* her to wear them.

---
### The Aftermath
- **Elara’s Choice**: Does she put the circlet on? Does she *refuse*?
- **Dain’s Game**: She’s *testing* Elara. She doesn’t know *why*—yet.
- **The Court’s Reaction**: They’re *watching*. They’re *always* watching.

---
**Final Note:**
This isn’t a morning after. It’s a *challenge*—and the thorns are *keeping score*.
