# Homework: Impossible Tasks

### 1. The Morning After
**Prompt**: Write the scene where Elara wakes up the morning after the wedding night.
- **Setting**: The bridal chamber. Dain is *already dressed*, watching her.
- **Key Details**:
  - What does Elara *say*?
  - What does Dain *not* say?
  - Do the thorns *react* to Elara’s *silence*?

---
### 2. Dain’s Hands
**Prompt**: Describe Dain’s hands in detail.
- **Key Details**:
  - Are the bandages *clean*?
  - Are there *thorns* growing *under* them?
  - Does Dain *peel* them off when angry—or *tighten* them?

---
### 3. The Library Secret
**Prompt**: What did Elara *save* from the library fire?
- **Key Details**:
  - It wasn’t books—it was something *smaller*.
  - A key? A letter? A *lock of hair*?
  - Why does Dain’s family *hate* it?

---
### 4. The Glass Knife
**Prompt**: Write the scene where Elara *uses* the glass knife.
- **Setting**: A court feast. Elara *demands* a weapon.
- **Key Details**:
  - The court gives her a *glass knife*.
  - It *cuts*—but it *shatters* when she tries to use it.
  - Does Dain *laugh*? Does the court *applaud*?

---
### 5. The True Name
**Prompt**: Write the moment Elara *speaks* Dain’s true name.
- **Setting**: The throne room. The court is *watching*.
- **Key Details**:
  - What *happens* when she speaks it?
  - Do the thorns *laugh*?
  - Does the *floor* open up beneath her?

---
### 6. The Thorned Circlet
**Prompt**: Write the first time Elara *wears* the circlet.
- **Setting**: A formal dinner. The court is *smirking*.
- **Key Details**:
  - Does it *whisper* to her?
  - Does it *draw blood*?
  - Does Dain *notice*?

---
**Final Note:**
These prompts are designed to *hurt*—in the best way. Pick one, and *make it bleed*.
