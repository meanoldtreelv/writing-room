# Unseelie Court Rules

### 1. No Direct Refusals
- If someone *demands* something, the court will *give* it—to their *detriment*.
  - Example: A guest asks for *wine*. They’re given a goblet—filled with *thorn-infused* vintage that *burns* on the way down.
- **Exception**: Dain can refuse, but only if she *replaces* the refusal with a *bargain*.

### 2. Gifts Are Curses
- Every gift comes with a *price*.
  - **Dain’s Wedding Gift to Elara**: A **thorned circlet** that *blooms* when she lies. The blooms *smell* like the library burning.
  - **The Court’s Gift to Dain**: Thorn-stitched bandages that *hide* her scars—but also *feed* the magic when she bleeds.
- **Rule**: The more *beautiful* the gift, the *deeper* the curse.

### 3. Names Have Power
- Dain’s true name is *forbidden*.
  - Her court calls her *Princip*—never anything else.
  - Elara *demands* to know it. Dain *smirks* and says, "Names are for people who *deserve* them."
- **Consequence**: Speaking her name *aloud* in the court will make the thorns *answer*.

### 4. Beauty is a Weapon
- The court is *stunning*—jewel-toned eyes, sharp smiles, clothes like spider silk.
- **But it hurts**:
  - Elara’s hands *bleed* from touching thorn-embroidered fabrics.
  - The *prettier* the courtier, the *more* their words cut.

### 5. The Rule of Three
- Every *favor* comes with a *debt*.
- Every *kindness* has a *barb*.
- Every *truth* is *incomplete*.

### 6. Thorn Magic Rules
- **Feeds on Pain**: The more Dain *hurts*, the stronger the magic.
- **Twists Truths**: The thorns *react* to lies—but they *reward* them.
- **The Bargain**: Dain’s power is tied to her *name*—and no one dares speak it aloud.

### 7. The Wedding Night Curse
- The bridal chamber is *too beautiful*.
- The bed is carved from black wood, the sheets embroidered with silver thorns.
- **The Air**: Smells like *honey and iron*.
- **The Thorns**: They *laugh*—a sound like breaking glass.

---
**Final Note:**
The Unseelie don’t *lie*. They just make sure the truth *hurts* more.
