# Thorn Magic

### Overview
The Veyne bloodline’s magic is tied to **thorns**—a cruel, sentient force that *feeds* on pain and *twists* the truth. It’s not just a power; it’s a *curse*, and it *chooses* its victims carefully.

---
### Mechanics
1. **Feeds on Pain**:
   - The more Dain *hurts* (physically or emotionally), the stronger the magic.
   - **Example**: Dain’s hands are wrapped in thorn-stitched bandages—not just to hide scars, but to *feed* the magic when she bleeds.

2. **Twists Truths**:
   - The thorns *react* to lies—but they don’t *punish* them. They *reward* them.
   - **Example**: If Elara lies, the thorns *bloom*. If Dain lies, the thorns *laugh*.

3. **The Bargain**:
   - Dain’s power is tied to her *name*—and no one in her court *dares* speak it aloud.
   - **Consequence**: Speaking her name *aloud* makes the thorns *answer*—and they’re not *kind*.

---
### Limitations
- **Only Works on Those Who *Love* Dain**:
  - But love is *complicated* when your partner *refuses* to be defined.
  - **Twist**: Elara’s bloodline is *immune* to the thorns. Dain doesn’t know why—yet.

- **The Cost**:
  - Every use of magic *draws blood*.
  - Dain’s bandages are *never* clean.

---
### The Thorned Circlet
- **Dain’s Wedding Gift to Elara**:
  - A circlet of silver thorns that *blooms* when she lies.
  - The blooms *smell* like the library burning.
  - **Curse**: The more Elara *wears* it, the more the thorns *grow* into her skin.

---
### The Court’s Reaction
- The court *knows* the rules.
- They *watch* Elara struggle.
- They *smile* when she bleeds.

---
**Final Note:**
Thorn magic isn’t just a power—it’s a *test*. And Dain and Elara are both *failing*.
