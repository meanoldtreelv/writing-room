# Princess Elara

### Overview
**Role**: The "sacrificial lamb" sent to marry Dain and secure the alliance. But she’s not as *innocent* as she seems.

---
### Public Face
- **The Dutiful Princess**: Willing to marry a stranger to save her kingdom.
- **The Romantic**: Believes in love stories—even when she’s *living* in one that’s *wrong*.
- **The Survivor**: Her kingdom is starving. She’s used to *hunger*—both literal and emotional.

---
### Secrets
1. **She’s the Spare**:
   - Not the heir. The *real* princess died in the library fire.
   - Her family *dressed her up* and sent her to die.
   - **Why It Matters**: She’s *expendable*—and she *knows* it.

2. **She Smuggled Survivors**:
   - The night the library burned, she helped *three people* escape.
   - Dain’s family would *execute* her for it if they found out.
   - **Why It Matters**: She’s not just a princess—she’s a *rebel*. And Dain’s court *hates* rebels.

3. **She’s Immune to Thorns**:
   - Her bloodline can *survive* thorn magic.
   - Dain doesn’t know why—yet.
   - **Why It Matters**: The thorns *like* her. And that’s *dangerous*.

---
### Motivations
- **Prove She’s Worthy**: She wants to *earn* her place—not just as a princess, but as a *leader*.
- **Uncover the Truth**: She *knows* she’s being lied to. She just doesn’t know *how much*.
- **Survive the Thorns**: The magic *wants* her. She doesn’t know if that’s a *gift* or a *curse*.

---
### Flaws
- **Stubborn**: She *refuses* to admit she’s wrong—even when she *is*.
- **Romantic**: She believes in *love*—and that’s her *weakness*.
- **Defiant**: She *insists* on calling Dain "him"—not because she believes it, but because *admitting she’s wrong feels like losing*.

---
### Relationships
- **Dain**: She *hates* that Dain *smirks* at her. She *hates* that Dain *knows* more than she does. She *hates* that she’s *attracted* to that.
- **Her Family**: They *lied* to her. They *used* her. She doesn’t know if she’ll ever *forgive* them.
- **The Court**: They *watch* her. They *laugh* at her. She doesn’t know *who* to trust.

---
### Key Scenes
1. **The Wedding Night**: The reveal. The smirk. The thorns *laughing*.
2. **The Circlet**: The first time she *wears* it—and realizes it’s *alive*.
3. **The Library Secret**: When Dain *finds out* what Elara did—and has to decide if it’s *betrayal* or *courage*.

---
**Final Note:**
Elara is *not* a damsel. She’s a *survivor*—and survivors *bite*.
