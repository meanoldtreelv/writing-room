# Field Marshal Valerius
**Role:** Antagonist/Love Interest, Architect of Change
**Age:** 28
**Appearance:**
- Tall, athletic, and imposing.
- Dark, closely cropped hair.
- Sharp, obsidian eyes that miss nothing.
- Prefers practical, form-fitting clothing (tunics, trousers, armor).
- A faint scar below her collarbone (from a past battle).
---
## Personality
**Core Traits:**
- **Disciplined**: Lives by a strict code of duty and honor.
- **Strategic**: Views everything as a tactical maneuver.
- **Unapologetic**: Defies expectations without hesitation.
- **Observant**: Notices details others overlook (e.g., Elara’s tension).

**Contradictions:**
- **Rigid Externally**: Follows Xylosian traditions to the letter.
- **Flexible Internally**: Adapts her methods to achieve her goals.
- **Cold in Public**: Warmth is reserved for those she trusts (a rare few).

**Lies She Tells Herself:**
- "I am only here for duty."
- "Elara is just another political piece."
- "I don’t care what Eldoria thinks of me."

**Wounds:**
- **Betrayal**: Has been let down by allies in the past.
- **Pressure**: The weight of Xylos’s expectations is crushing.
- **Loneliness**: Few understand her dual role as warrior and diplomat.
---
## Motivations
**Primary:**
- To secure Xylos’s influence over Eldoria.
- To prove her worth to the Emperor.

**Secondary:**
- To challenge Eldoria’s outdated traditions.
- To reshape Eldoria in Xylos’s image.

**Secret:**
- Fascinated by Elara’s quiet strength and hidden fire.
---
## Relationships
**Emperor of Xylos (Father):**
- Respects him but feels the weight of his expectations.
- Sees herself as his most loyal tool.

**Elara (Betrothed):**
- Initially views her as a pawn in the alliance.
- Growing intrigued by her intelligence and resilience.
- Conflicted by her attraction to her.

**Xylosian Court:**
- Feared and respected, but not truly understood.
- Seen as the Emperor’s enforcer.
---
## Arc
**Beginning:**
- Arrives in Eldoria as a disciplined, detached Field Marshal.

**Middle:**
- Begins to see Elara as more than a political asset.
- Struggles with her growing feelings for Elara.

**End:**
- Must choose between her duty to Xylos and her heart.
- Helps Elara embrace her power as Queen.
---
## Key Scenes
- **Arrival**: Stuns the Eldorian court by revealing her gender.
- **Sword Drill**: Knows Elara is watching; performs with deliberate precision.
- **Private Conversation**: Challenges Elara’s assumptions about her.
- **Coronation**: Supports Elara as she takes the throne.
---
## Notes
- **Voice**: Clear, commanding, and surprisingly melodic.
- **Symbolism**: Represents the "storm"—disruptive but transformative.
- **Growth**: Learns to value Elara’s perspective and softens her rigid worldview.
