# Unseelie Influence: Plot Beats

### Overview
The Unseelie Court’s rules shape the story’s conflict, magic, and *cruelty*. Here’s how they play out:

---
### Act 1: The Wedding
1. **The Lie**: Elara is told she’s marrying a *man*.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: Dain’s court *knows* she’s not. They *let* Elara believe it.
2. **The Gift**: Dain gives Elara the **thorned circlet**.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: It *blooms* when she lies. The blooms *smell* like the library burning.
3. **The Warning**: "Thorns *listen*."
   - **Unseelie Twist**: They do. And they *remember*.

---
### Act 2: The Game
1. **The Glass Knife**: Elara asks for a *weapon*. The court gives her one—*made of glass*.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: It *cuts*—but it *shatters* when she tries to use it.
2. **The Name**: Elara *demands* Dain’s true name.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: Dain *smirks* and says, "Names are for people who *deserve* them."
3. **The Blood**: Dain’s bandages *bleed*.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: The thorns *feed* on it. The magic *grows*.

---
### Act 3: The Reckoning
1. **The Truth**: Elara *speaks* Dain’s true name.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: The thorns *answer*. The court *laughs*.
2. **The Choice**: Elara must *prove* she’s worthy.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: The thorns *choose* her. The court *hates* it.
3. **The Cost**: Love is the *price*.
   - **Unseelie Twist**: But love is *complicated* when your partner *refuses* to be defined.

---
### Key Scenes
1. **The Glass Knife**: Elara *bleeds*—and realizes the court *wanted* her to.
2. **The Name**: When Elara *speaks* it—and the thorns *answer*.
3. **The Circlet**: When Elara *wears* it—and the thorns *grow* into her skin.

---
**Final Note:**
The Unseelie don’t *lie*. They just make sure the truth *hurts* more.
