# A Cage Of Thorns: Vault Guide

### Overview
This folder contains all notes, lore, and scenes for *A Cage Of Thorns*—a dark romantasy with **Unseelie influence**, **thorn magic**, and a **pronoun war** at its heart.

---
### Structure
- **`Story Pitch.md`**: The premise, themes, and inspirations.
- **`Lore/`**: Worldbuilding and magic rules.
  - `Unseelie Court Rules.md`: The court’s cruelty and glamour.
  - `Thorn Magic.md`: How the magic works—and what it *costs*.
- **`Characters/`**: Deep dives on Elara and Dain.
  - `Princess Elara.md`: Her secrets, motivations, and flaws.
  - `Princip Dain.md`: Her power, pronouns, and thorned hands.
- **`Scenes/`**: Key moments in the story.
  - `Wedding Night.md`: The reveal, the smirk, the thorns *laughing*.
- **`Plots/`**: Story arcs and Unseelie influence.
  - `Unseelie Influence.md`: Plot beats tied to the court’s rules.
- **`Homework.md`**: "Impossible Tasks" to push the story further.

---
### Key Rules
1. **No Direct Refusals**: The court gives you what you *ask for*—just not what you *need*.
2. **Gifts Are Curses**: Every present comes with a *price*.
3. **Names Have Power**: Dain’s true name is *forbidden*—and speaking it has *consequences*.
4. **Thorns Listen**: They *react* to lies, to pain, to *truths* you’d rather hide.

---
### Writing Tips
- **Subtext Over Dialogue**: If a character says what they mean, *rewrite it*.
- **Make It Hurt**: Every scene should have a *cost*—emotional, physical, or magical.
- **The Pronoun War**: Elara’s insistence on "him" is *defiance*. Dain’s smirk is *control*.
- **Unseelie Glamour**: Beauty should *cut*. Kindness should *bite*.

---
### For Coauthors
- **Pick a Prompt**: Start with one of the "Impossible Tasks" in `Homework.md`.
- **Expand a Scene**: Add to `Wedding Night.md` or draft a new one.
- **Ask Questions**: If something’s unclear, *lean into it*. The Unseelie thrive on ambiguity.

---
**Final Note:**
This isn’t a love story. It’s a *bargain*—and the thorns always *win*.
