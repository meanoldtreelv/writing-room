MGS is an interesting proect. its 1 part stardew valley, 1 part princess raising sim, 1 part harem fantasy, and 3 parts niche genre fiction. This novel isnt being written to appeal to everyone. instead its being written because its the kind of story i enjoy reading. It follows the same Harvest Moon / Stardew Valley story trope: estranged granpa dies, leaves you his farm, you leave your life behind in the busy city because you want a slower pace, there is someting literally magical about the farm and you find yourself integrated into town like you always belonged.
