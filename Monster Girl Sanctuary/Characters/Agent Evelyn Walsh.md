# Agent Evelyn Walsh

**Role:** Agent, Federal Bureau of Extraordinary Phenomena
**Era of Origin:** Modern-day
**Appearance:** Impeccably dressed in a severe grey suit, exudes professionalism and authority. Her expression is often unreadable, adding to her air of mystery.

**Personality:**
- **Professional:** Takes her job seriously and maintains a composed demeanor.
- **Secretive:** Used to dealing with classified information, she is cautious about what she reveals.
- **Empathetic:** Despite her professional exterior, she shows genuine concern for Alvis's well-being.
- **Dutiful:** Committed to her role in protecting the secrets of the BMZ.

**Contradictions:**
- **Authority vs. Compassion:** Balances her duty to maintain secrecy with her empathy for those affected by the BMZ.

**Motivations:**
- **Maintaining Secrecy:** Ensuring the BMZ remains hidden from the general public.
- **Guiding Newcomers:** Helping individuals like Alvis transition into their new roles within the BMZ.

**Wounds:**
- **Burden of Knowledge:** Carries the weight of knowing about the magical world while having to keep it secret.

**Relationships:**
- **Alvis Anders:** Sees her as someone who needs guidance and protection as she enters the BMZ.
- **BMZ Community:** Works to protect and support the residents of the BMZ.
