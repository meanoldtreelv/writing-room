# Agent Olivia Tiller

**Role:** Agent, Special Reconnaissance Division of the BMZ Program
**Era of Origin:** Modern-day
**Appearance:** Dressed in a tailored black suit, with a mischievous spark in her eyes. Her black hair is tied up in a high ponytail, giving her a youthful and energetic look.

**Personality:**
- **Confident:** Comfortable in her role and knowledgeable about the BMZ.
- **Friendly:** Approachable and supportive, she helps Alvis feel at ease.
- **Mischievous:** Enjoys the adventure and mystery of the BMZ.
- **Protective:** Takes her role as a guide seriously, ensuring Alvis's safety.

**Contradictions:**
- **Professional vs. Playful:** Balances her professional duties with a playful and adventurous spirit.

**Motivations:**
- **Guiding Alvis:** Helping her navigate the BMZ and understand its complexities.
- **Protecting the BMZ:** Ensuring the safety and secrecy of the BMZ and its inhabitants.

**Wounds:**
- **Responsibility:** Feels the weight of her role in guiding newcomers and protecting the BMZ.

**Relationships:**
- **Alvis Anders:** Acts as her guide and ally, helping her adapt to life in the BMZ.
- **BMZ Community:** Well-respected within the BMZ, she has strong relationships with its residents.
