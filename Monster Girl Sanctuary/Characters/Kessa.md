# Kessa
**Species:** Lamia (With a *bite* that’s more than venom.)
**Role:** The Rebel / Vexis’s Prodigy
**Age:** *Young enough to still believe in escape.*

---

## Appearance
- **Hair:** Black as oil, streaked with *gold*—the color of the coins she’s stolen.
- **Eyes:** Pupils are *slits*, like a cat’s, but they *dilate* when she lies.
- **Tail:** Barbed, but the venom isn’t lethal. It’s a *truth serum*. (She uses it *often*.)
- **Voice:** A purr with *teeth* in it.

---

## Personality
- **The Thief:** She steals *everything*—coins, secrets, glances.
- **The Student:** Vexis taught her to read the Ledger. She’s been *editing* it.
- **The Hungry:** Not for food. For *leverage*.

**Key Traits:**
- **Cunning:** She plays the fool. She’s *never* the fool.
- **Loyal (To a Point):** She’d die for Vexis. But she’d *betray* her first.
- **Bored:** The Sanctuary is a cage. She’s been *pickpocketing* the keys.

---

## Abilities
1. **The Bite:** Her venom doesn’t kill. It *unravels*. A drop on the tongue, and you’ll tell her your darkest secret—*willingly*.
2. **The Slip:** She can *phase* through shadows, but only for a few seconds. Long enough to steal. Not long enough to *escape*.
3. **The Whisper:** She can mimic any voice she’s heard. She uses this to *frame* others.

---

## Secrets
- She’s been *siphoning* time from the Ledger. Not much—just seconds here and there. But it *adds up*.
- She knows the protagonist’s mark is a *fragment* of Vexis’s name. She’s *testing* him to see if he knows.
- She’s in love with Ylva. (Ylva *knows*. Ylva *doesn’t care*.)

---

## Relationships
### With the Protagonist
- **Public:** She *teases* him. Relentlessly.
- **Private:** She *tests* him. (How much can he take? How much will he *give*?)
- **Truth:** She wants to *use* him to escape. Or *break* him trying.

### With Vexis
- **Adoration & Resentment:** Vexis *made* her. That doesn’t mean she’ll stay *made*.

### With Ylva
- **Obsession & Disdain:** Ylva is the only one who’s ever *seen* her. That’s why she can’t *have* her.

---

## Dialogue Tics
- Calls the protagonist "Pet." (He hates it. She *knows*.)
- Licks her fangs when she’s *lying*. (They’re always *damp*.)
- Quotes Vexis—*wrong*. Just to see who notices.

---

## Key Scenes
1. **The First Bite:** She "accidentally" poisons him. The truth he spills? *He’s been in the Sanctuary before.*
2. **The Ledger Heist:** She catches him *reading* it. Blackmails him into helping her *steal* from it.
3. **The Betrayal:** She sells him out to Vexis. Then *regrets* it. (Maybe.)

---

## Weaknesses
- **Ylva:** The only one who’s ever *refused* her.
- **The Ledger:** It’s *watching* her. She doesn’t know how much it *knows*.
- **Her Own Venom:** She’s *immune* to the truth serum. But the *withdrawal* when it wears off? *Agony*.

---

## The Lyra Note
Kessa is the *knife* in the dark. She’s sharp, she’s *hungry*, and she doesn’t care if she *cuts* herself in the process.

**Final Line:**
"You want to know why I bite?" She grins, fangs glinting. "Because *teeth* are the only thing they can’t take from me."
