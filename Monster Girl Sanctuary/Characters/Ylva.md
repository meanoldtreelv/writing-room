# Ylva
**Species:** Harpy (But her wings aren’t for flight—they’re for *remembering*.)
**Role:** The Outcast / Former Hunter
**Age:** *Old enough to know better. Young enough to do it anyway.*

---

## Appearance
- **Hair:** White as bone, braided with *feathers*—each one from a different kill.
- **Eyes:** Gold, like a hawk’s. They don’t blink.
- **Wings:** Not for flying. For *touching*. Each feather *records* what it brushes against—skin, stone, or *sin*.
- **Voice:** A rasp, like wind through dead branches.

---

## Personality
- **The Hunter:** She was *exiled* for hunting *too well*.
- **The Archivist:** Her wings are a *library* of sensations. She *collects* secrets like others collect coins.
- **The Cynic:** She believes in two things: *blood* and *bargains*.

**Key Traits:**
- **Ruthless:** She’ll gut you if it’s *efficient*.
- **Loyal (To the Dead):** She keeps the feathers of those she’s killed. *Some* of them didn’t deserve it.
- **Curious:** She’s the only one who’s ever *asked* the protagonist about his mark. That’s why she hasn’t killed him. *Yet*.

---

## Abilities
1. **The Archive:** Her wings *remember* every touch. She can *replay* sensations—pleasure, pain, or *lies*.
2. **The Scream:** A shriek that *shatters* glass—and *resolve*.
3. **The Fall:** She can *drop* from any height, unharmed. The landing? *That’s* when she strikes.

---

## Secrets
- She was the one who *brought* the protagonist to the Sanctuary. (She doesn’t remember *why*.)
- Her wings are *mapping* the Sanctuary’s layout. She’s looking for a way *out*—or a way *in*.
- She’s the only one who’s ever *seen* Vexis cry. (She didn’t *tell* anyone. But she *recorded* it.)

---

## Relationships
### With the Protagonist
- **Public:** She *ignores* him.
- **Private:** She *watches* him.
- **Truth:** She *recognizes* his mark. It’s the same as the one on the *first* human she ever killed.

### With Vexis
- **Respect & Contempt:** Vexis *spared* her. That’s the only reason she’s still alive. (She *hates* owing debts.)

### With Kessa
- **Pity & Desire:** Kessa is the only one who’s ever *touched* her wings *gently*. That’s why she’ll never *trust* her.

---

## Dialogue Tics
- Speaks in *metaphors*—but only because the truth would *break* the listener.
- Calls the protagonist "Ghost." (He doesn’t know why.)
- When she’s *lying*, her feathers *rustle*—even when there’s no wind.

---

## Key Scenes
1. **The Recognition:** She *sees* his mark. Her reaction isn’t horror. It’s *recognition*.
2. **The Archive:** She lets him *touch* her wings. The sensations he feels aren’t his.
3. **The Choice:** She offers to help him escape. The price? *A feather from his future.*

---

## Weaknesses
- **Her Wings:** They’re her strength—and her *curse*. If they’re damaged, she loses *memories*.
- **The First Kill:** The mark on the protagonist matches the one on the human she *failed* to kill. That failure *haunts* her.
- **Kessa:** The only one who’s ever *seen* her without armor. (She *hates* that.)

---

## The Lyra Note
Ylva is the *storm* on the horizon. She’s coming. The question is whether she’ll *save* him—or *bury* him.

**Final Line:**
"You think you’re the first human to wear that mark?" Her laugh is the sound of *bones* snapping. "Oh, little ghost. You’re not even the first this *month*."
