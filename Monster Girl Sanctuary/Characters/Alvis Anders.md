# Alvis Anders

**Role:** Protagonist, Inheritor of Anders Farm
**Era of Origin:** Modern-day New York City
**Appearance:** Professional, likely in her late 20s or early 30s. Dresses in business attire, reflecting her finance career. Her appearance is polished but carries a hint of weariness from her high-stress job.

**Personality:**
- **Ambitious:** Successful in her finance career, driven, and focused.
- **Analytical:** Good at problem-solving and assessing situations logically.
- **Cautious:** Initially skeptical of the magical elements she encounters.
- **Adaptable:** Despite her initial reluctance, she begins to adapt to the new world of the BMZ.
- **Compassionate:** Shows empathy and concern for others, especially as she gets to know the residents of Meadowbrook.

**Contradictions:**
- **Logical vs. Magical:** Struggles to reconcile her logical, finance-oriented mindset with the magical reality of the BMZ.
- **Independence vs. Belonging:** While she values her independence, she finds a sense of belonging in Meadowbrook.

**Motivations:**
- **Understanding her Inheritance:** Wants to uncover the truth about her grandfather's farm and why he left it to her.
- **Finding Purpose:** Seeks a deeper meaning in her life beyond her finance career.
- **Protecting the BMZ:** As she learns more, she feels a responsibility to protect the BMZ and its inhabitants.

**Wounds:**
- **Family History:** Has unresolved feelings about her family, particularly her grandfather and her parents' divorce.
- **Isolation:** Despite her success, she feels isolated in her New York life.

**Relationships:**
- **Grandfather (Robert Anders):** A figure from her past, she has fond but distant memories of him and his farm.
- **Agent Evelyn Walsh:** Initially sees her as an authority figure but grows to trust her.
- **Agent Olivia Tiller:** Her guide and ally in the BMZ, who helps her navigate the new world.
- **Whisper:** A mysterious figure from her grandfather's past who becomes a friend and mentor.
- **Mrs. Vee:** A welcoming presence in Meadowbrook who helps Alvis feel at home.
