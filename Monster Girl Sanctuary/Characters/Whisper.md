# Whisper

**Role:** Mysterious Being from the BMZ, Friend of Robert Anders
**Era of Origin:** BMZ (timeless)
**Appearance:** Small, with shimmering chestnut hair and brown skin that blends with the earthy tones of the forest. She has deer-like ears and a graceful, almost ethereal presence.

**Personality:**
- **Wise:** Knows much about the BMZ and its history.
- **Compassionate:** Shows genuine care and concern for Alvis, helping her feel at home.
- **Mysterious:** Has a deep connection to the BMZ and its secrets.
- **Playful:** Enjoys the wonder and magic of the BMZ, often with a hint of mischief.

**Contradictions:**
- **Mystery vs. Openness:** While she is mysterious, she is also open and welcoming to Alvis.

**Motivations:**
- **Honoring Robert Anders:** Wants to preserve his legacy and ensure his farm is well cared for.
- **Guiding Alvis:** Helps her understand and adapt to the BMZ.

**Wounds:**
- **Loss:** Feels the absence of Robert Anders and the past they shared.

**Relationships:**
- **Alvis Anders:** Acts as a mentor and friend, helping her connect with her grandfather's past.
- **Robert Anders:** Had a close friendship with him, sharing many memories.
- **BMZ Community:** Well-loved and respected within Meadowbrook.
