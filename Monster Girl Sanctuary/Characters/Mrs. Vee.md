# Mrs. Vee

**Role:** Resident of Meadowbrook, Baker
**Era of Origin:** BMZ (longtime resident)
**Appearance:** An old woman with wrinkled features and kind, twinkling blue eyes. She exudes warmth and wisdom.

**Personality:**
- **Welcoming:** Makes Alvis feel at home in Meadowbrook.
- **Wise:** Knows much about the BMZ and its history.
- **Kind:** Shows genuine care and concern for others.
- **Playful:** Enjoys sharing stories and the magic of the BMZ.

**Contradictions:**
- **Wisdom vs. Playfulness:** Balances her deep knowledge of the BMZ with a playful and lighthearted demeanor.

**Motivations:**
- **Community:** Wants to ensure Meadowbrook remains a welcoming and safe place.
- **Preserving Traditions:** Values the traditions and history of the BMZ.

**Wounds:**
- **Loss of Old Friends:** Feels the absence of those who have passed, like Robert Anders.

**Relationships:**
- **Alvis Anders:** Welcomes her warmly and helps her feel at home.
- **BMZ Community:** Well-respected and loved within Meadowbrook.
