# Vexis
**Species:** Gorgon (But not the kind you’ve read about.)
**Role:** Matriarch of the Sanctuary
**Age:** *Old enough to remember when the walls were bones.*

---

## Appearance
- **Hair:** Not snakes—*shadows*. They move when she isn’t, whispering in languages that haven’t existed for centuries.
- **Eyes:** The irises are *clocks*, their hands moving backward. Looking into them doesn’t turn you to stone. It turns you to *memory*.
- **Skin:** Pale as marble, but *warm*—like a corpse that hasn’t realized it’s dead.
- **Voice:** A chorus of three: her own, a child’s, and something that sounds like a *door* creaking open.

---

## Personality
- **The Ruler:** She didn’t *take* power. The Sanctuary *gave* it to her, and it’s been *eating* her ever since.
- **The Scholar:** She collects secrets like other women collect jewelry. The more dangerous, the better.
- **The Hungry:** She doesn’t eat food. She eats *time*—stealing moments from others to feed her own longevity.

**Key Traits:**
- **Patience:** She’ll wait a decade to make a point.
- **Cruelty:** Not because she enjoys it, but because *kindness is a luxury she can’t afford*.
- **Loneliness:** She’s the oldest. She’s *always* the oldest.

---

## Abilities
1. **The Gaze:** Doesn’t petrify—it *rewrites*. A glance can make you forget your name. A stare can make you remember *hers*.
2. **The Ledger:** A living book of every debt, sin, and favor in the Sanctuary. It *bleeds* when lies are written in it.
3. **The Heartbeat:** She can *sync* her pulse with the Sanctuary’s. When she’s angry, the walls *bleed*.

---

## Secrets
- She *marked* the protagonist. The sigil on his skin is a *fragment* of her own name.
- She’s been *stealing* time from the other monster girls to keep herself alive. They don’t know. *Yet.*
- The Sanctuary isn’t a refuge. It’s a *prison*. She’s the warden—and the only inmate who knows the doors are *locked from the outside*.

---

## Relationships
### With the Protagonist
- **Public:** She *tolerates* him. Barely.
- **Private:** She *watches* him. Always.
- **Truth:** She *needs* him. His mark is the key to something—or the lock.

### With Kessa (Lamia)
- **Mentor & Tormentor:** She *groomed* Kessa to be her successor. Then Kessa started asking *questions*.

### With Ylva (Harpy)
- **Respect & Resentment:** Ylva is the only one who *remembers* what the Sanctuary was before Vexis. That makes her *dangerous*.

---

## Dialogue Tics
- Speaks in *riddles*—but only because the truth would *break* the listener.
- Calls the protagonist "Little Thief." (He stole something from her. He doesn’t remember what.)
- Humming: When she’s *lying*, she hums a lullaby from a cradle that no longer exists.

---

## Key Scenes
1. **The Marking:** The moment she *brands* him. It’s not just ink. It’s a *promise*.
2. **The Ledger:** She forces him to read it. The pages *bite*.
3. **The Confession:** When she admits she’s *afraid*—not of him, but of what he’ll *become*.

---

## Weaknesses
- **The Heart:** The Sanctuary’s core *hates* her. It tolerates her because she’s *useful*, but it would *drown* her if it could.
- **The Past:** She remembers *everything*. That’s the problem.
- **Him:** The protagonist is the first thing in centuries she hasn’t been able to *control*.

---

## The Lyra Note
Vexis isn’t a villain. She’s a *warden*. The question is whether she’s keeping the monster girls *safe*—or *trapped*.

**Final Line:**
"You think you’re the first human to wear my mark? Darling, you’re not even the *first* this week. But you might be the first to *survive* it."
