# STYLE RUBRIC: Monster Girl Sanctuary

## Scoring System (1-9)
Each scene will be graded on the following metrics. **Aim for 7+ in all categories.**

---

### 1. Visceral Impact (Sensory & Physicality)
**1-3:** Clinical. "She had scales."
**4-6:** Descriptive. "Her scales were iridescent, shifting like oil on water."
**7-9:** *Immersive.* "Her scales weren’t just *there*—they *caught* the light, then *ate* it, leaving his fingers smeared with darkness when he pulled away."

**Key Question:** Can the reader *feel* the scene?

---

### 2. Power Dynamics (Agency & Conflict)
**1-3:** Passive. "They had sex."
**4-6:** Active. "She pinned him down."
**7-9:** *Negotiated.* "She pinned him down—not because she could, but because he *let* her, and that surrender was the only leverage he had left."

**Key Question:** Who’s *really* in control, and what are they *hiding*?

---

### 3. The Lie Quotient (Deception & Omission)
**1-3:** Honest. "I love you," she said.
**4-6:** Evading. "You’re *useful*," she said.
**7-9:** *Layered.* "You’re *useful*," she said, and he knew—because of the way her tail *twitched*—that she meant something else entirely."

**Key Question:** What’s the *real* conversation happening beneath the words?

---

### 4. Worldbuilding Integration (Lore & Stakes)
**1-3:** Generic. "They fucked in a cave."
**4-6:** Contextual. "They fucked in the cave where her clan had *buried* his kind for centuries."
**7-9:** *Consequential.* "They fucked in the cave where her clan had buried his kind—and every gasp echoed like a *violation* of the truce."

**Key Question:** How does this scene *change* the world or the characters’ place in it?

---

### 5. The Prose Scalpel (Precision & Originality)
**1-3:** Cliché. "Her touch was electric."
**4-6:** Fresh. "Her touch wasn’t a spark—it was a *current*, rewiring his nerves to *her* frequency."
**7-9:** *Unsettling.* "Her touch didn’t just *tingle*—it *rearranged*, like someone had taken a scalpel to his memories and *stitched* her into the gaps."

**Key Question:** Have I *ever* read this exact phrase before?

---

### 6. The Aftermath (Consequence & Lingering Effects)
**1-3:** None. "They cuddled after."
**4-6:** Physical. "He had bruises."
**7-9:** *Metaphysical.* "He had bruises—and when he touched them, he *heard* her voice in his skull, humming a lullaby from a language that didn’t exist yet."

**Key Question:** What *lingers* after the scene ends?

---

## Scoring Examples

### Example 1: A Failing Scene (Averages 2-3)
> "Lilia the lamia wrapped her tail around his waist as they kissed. It felt nice. Afterward, they talked about their feelings."
- **Visceral Impact:** 2 ("felt nice" is *nothing*.)
- **Power Dynamics:** 1 (No tension.)
- **The Lie Quotient:** 1 (Honest to a fault.)
- **Worldbuilding Integration:** 1 (Could be any generic fantasy.)
- **The Prose Scalpel:** 2 (Bland.)
- **The Aftermath:** 1 (No consequences.)

**Verdict:** *Delete it.*

---

### Example 2: A Passing Scene (Averages 5-6)
> "Lilia’s tail coiled around his throat—not tight enough to choke, but *enough* to remind him who held the leverage. Her venom burned where it seeped into the cuts on his lips, a slow fire that made his confession *easier*. When she pulled away, his voice was hoarse, and not just from shouting."
- **Visceral Impact:** 6 (Venom, cuts, hoarse voice—good, but could be *sharper*.)
- **Power Dynamics:** 7 (Tail as leverage, confession under duress.)
- **The Lie Quotient:** 5 (Implied deception, but could be deeper.)
- **Worldbuilding Integration:** 5 (Venom as truth serum is *interesting*, but needs more lore ties.)
- **The Prose Scalpel:** 6 (Strong, but "slow fire" is a bit cliché.)
- **The Aftermath:** 6 (Hoarse voice lingers, but what *else*?)

**Verdict:** *Revise.* Needs more *bite*.

---

### Example 3: A Masterful Scene (Averages 8-9)
> "The first time Vexis let him *see* her true form, the air *thickened*—not with magic, but with the weight of a thousand unspoken bargains. Her wings weren’t just *there*; they *moved*, each feather a blade tracing patterns in the dust, spelling out words in a language his eyes couldn’t follow but his *skin* remembered. When she bit him—*finally*—it wasn’t for blood. It was for the *sound* he made, the one that matched the frequency of the Sanctuary’s heart. The mark she left didn’t bleed. It *pulsed*."
- **Visceral Impact:** 9 (Feathers as blades, skin remembering language, *pulsing* mark.)
- **Power Dynamics:** 8 (He’s *allowed* to see her, but the cost is implicit.)
- **The Lie Quotient:** 9 (Unspoken bargains, language his eyes can’t follow.)
- **Worldbuilding Integration:** 9 (Ties to the Sanctuary’s heart, lore-heavy.)
- **The Prose Scalpel:** 9 (Original, vivid, *cutting*.)
- **The Aftermath:** 9 (A *pulsing* mark—what does it *do*?)

**Verdict:** *Publishable.*

---

## The Revision Checklist
If a scene scores below 7 in any category, ask:
1. **What’s Missing?** (Sensory details? Conflict? Stakes?)
2. **What’s Cliché?** (Replace it with something *specific* to your world.)
3. **What’s the Lie?** (If everyone’s honest, *rewrite it*.)
4. **What Lingers?** (If nothing, *add a cost*.)

---

**Final Rule:**
If you’re not *uncomfortable* writing it, you’re not *trying* hard enough.
