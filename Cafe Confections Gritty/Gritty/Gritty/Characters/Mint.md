# Mint
**Role:** Hostess, "Spearmint" Flavor
**Age:** 18
**Appearance:**
- **Tiny and frail**, with a **flat chest** and **long, bright green twin tails**.
- **Pale skin** and **large, nervous eyes** that dart away from direct contact.
- Wears **pastel green outfits** (often frilly or schoolgirl-style).
---
## Personality
**Core Traits:**
- **Timid**: Flinches at loud noises, avoids confrontation.
- **Submissive**: Follows instructions **without question**, even if uncomfortable.
- **Childlike**: Speaks softly, uses **polite honorifics** (calls patrons "Master").
- **Loyal**: **Clings to Cara** as her only source of safety.

**Contradictions:**
- **Innocent Act**: Plays the **"shy little sister"** role but **knows exactly what’s expected** of her.
- **Self-Harm**: Cuts her thighs when stressed but **hides it under her skirt**.
- **Survivor’s Instinct**: **Too scared to rebel**, but **notices everything** (e.g., the Buffet Door).

**Lies She Tells Herself:**
- "If I’m good, they’ll let me leave someday."
- "The manager protects me."
- "I don’t mind the work."

**Wounds:**
- **Abuse**: **Orphaned at 12**, abused in a **group home** (staff sold her to the café).
- **Trauma**: **Dissociates** during services, pretends she’s "somewhere else."
- **Fear of the Buffet Door**: **Heard screams** from inside; **terrified of being sent there**.
---
## Motivations
**Primary:**
- To **avoid punishment** (e.g., being sent to the Buffet Door).
- To **please Cara** (her only "friend").

**Secondary:**
- To **earn enough to leave** (though she **doesn’t know how**).
- To **protect herself** from patrons who **get too rough**.

**Secret:**
- **Wishes she could disappear**—fantasizes about **running away with Cara**.
---
## Relationships
**Cara:**
- **Dynamic**: **Big sister/little sister** bond. Cara is her **only ally**.
- **Trust Level**: **Absolute**—she’d **die for Cara**.

**Coco:**
- **Dynamic**: **Intimidated** by her confidence.
- **Trust Level**: **Low**—thinks Coco is **too selfish** to help her.

**Custard:**
- **Dynamic**: **Resents her** for acting "tough."
- **Trust Level**: **None**—thinks Custard is **fake**.

**Manager:**
- **Dynamic**: **Terrified** of them; they **threaten her with the Buffet Door**.
- **Trust Level**: **-10**—sees them as a **monster**.

**Patrons:**
- **Dynamic**: **Obeys without question**, even if **disgusted** by them.
- **Trust Level**: **None**—but **pretends to trust** them for survival.
---
## Arc
**Beginning:**
- A **broken, obedient girl** who **doesn’t question her role**.

**Middle:**
- Starts **noticing cracks** in the café’s façade (e.g., Custard’s blackmail, the Buffet Door).
- **Bonds with David** if he shows her **kindness** (but **fears getting attached**).

**End:**
- **Forced to choose**: Stay safe in the café or **risk everything to escape** with Cara.
- If she **ends up in the Buffet Door**, it **breaks her completely**.
---
## Key Scenes
- **First Meeting with David**: **Flinches** when he touches her but **forces herself to smile**.
- **Self-Harm Discovery**: Cara **finds her cuts** and **bandages them in secret**.
- **Buffet Door Threat**: The manager **drags her toward it** as punishment; she **screams for Cara**.
- **Escape Attempt**: If she **runs**, she’s **caught and punished**—or **disappears forever**.
---
## Notes
- **Voice**: **Whispery, stuttering**, but **sharp when cornered**.
- **Symbolism**: The **"frail mint"**—sweet on the outside, **broken inside**.
- **Growth**: Learns to **trust her instincts**—or **loses herself completely**.
