# Cafe Confections: Operations
---
## Overview
Cafe Confections is a **hostess café** hidden in Tokyo’s underground shopping district near Shibuya Station. It operates under the guise of a dessert café but offers **exclusive, coded services** to its patrons.

### Key Features:
- **Location**: Underground, accessible via cryptic directions (intentionally hard to find).
- **Atmosphere**: Wood-paneled lobby with red velvet benches, designed to feel intimate and luxurious.
- **Clientèle**: Elite or connected individuals (businessmen, yakuza, or underground figures).

3. Service is performed per the "dish" rules.
4. Patron leaves or moves to the **Buffet Door** (see below).
