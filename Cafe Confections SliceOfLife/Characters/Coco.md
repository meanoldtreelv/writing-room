# Chocolate (Coco)
**Role:** Hostess, "Chocolate" Flavor
**Age:** 21
**Appearance:**
- **Small but curvy**, with **long, curly dark brown hair**.
- **Dark skin** and **golden-brown eyes** that **smolder when angry**.
- Wears **burgundy and brown outfits** (often tight or revealing).
---
## Personality
**Core Traits:**
- **Confident**: **Owns her sexuality**, uses it as a **weapon**.
- **Dominant**: **Takes charge** during services, **demands respect**.
- **Cynical**: **Hates the café** but **plays the game** to survive.
- **Loyal to No One**: **Looks out for herself first**—but **has a soft spot for Mint**.

**Contradictions:**
- **Tough Exterior**: Acts **unbreakable**, but **steals from patrons** to save money.
- **Secret Softness**: **Pretends not to care**, but **notices when someone is kind**.
- **Self-Sabotage**: **Tests boundaries** (e.g., stealing, talking back) but **knows she’ll get caught**.

**Lies She Tells Herself:**
- "I’m in control here."
- "I don’t need anyone."
- "I’ll escape someday."

**Wounds:**
- **Betrayal**: **Half-Japanese, half-Nigerian**; disowned by her **father’s family** for being "too foreign."
- **Survivor’s Guilt**: **Mother died** when she was 19; **blames herself** for not being there.
- **Trapped**: **Chose the café** over homelessness but **hates herself for it**.
---
## Motivations
**Primary:**
- To **save enough money to leave Japan** (dreams of moving to **Okinawa or America**).
- To **protect Mint** (the only one she **genuinely cares about**).

**Secondary:**
- To **punish patrons** who **disrespect her** (e.g., stealing from them).
- To **prove she’s not weak** (even if it **hurts her**).

**Secret:**
- **Wants to burn the café down**—but **won’t risk Mint’s safety**.
---
## Relationships
**Cara:**
- **Dynamic**: **Respects her** but **thinks she’s naive**.
- **Trust Level**: **Moderate**—would **help her in a crisis**.

**Mint:**
- **Dynamic**: **Protective** (sees her as a **little sister**).
- **Trust Level**: **High**—but **won’t let her see her steal**.

**Custard:**
- **Dynamic**: **Mutual respect**—both are **survivors**.
- **Trust Level**: **Low**—thinks Custard is **too reckless**.

**Manager:**
- **Dynamic**: **Openly despises them** but **plays nice** to avoid trouble.
- **Trust Level**: **-10**—**would stab them if she could**.

**Patrons:**
- **Dynamic**: **Manipulates them** (e.g., **flirts to distract while stealing**).
- **Trust Level**: **None**—but **pretends to enjoy their company**.
---
## Arc
**Beginning:**
- A **jaded hostess** who **uses her charm to survive**.

**Middle:**
- **Bonds with David** if he **treats her like a person**—but **tests his loyalty**.
- **Discovers Custard’s blackmail** and **considers using it** to escape.

**End:**
- **Forced to choose**: **Burn the café down** (risking Mint) or **stay trapped**.
- If she **escapes**, she **takes Mint with her**—or **leaves her behind**.
---
## Key Scenes
- **First Meeting with David**: **Flirts aggressively** but **watches his reactions closely**.
- **Stealing from a Patron**: **Gets caught**; the manager **beats her** as punishment.
- **Confronting Custard**: **Argues with her** about her blackmail scheme—**"You’ll get us all killed!"**
- **Escape Plan**: **Packing a bag** when she hears **Mint was taken to the Buffet Door**.
---
## Notes
- **Voice**: **Sarcastic, bold**, but **quiet when vulnerable**.
- **Symbolism**: **"Dark chocolate"**—bitter on the outside, **rich and complex inside**.
- **Growth**: Learns to **trust others**—or **becomes as ruthless as the manager**.
