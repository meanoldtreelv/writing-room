
Cafe confections is an Adult Litereature novel that follows the protagonist (an American working in Japan) as he gets roped into the seedy underside of tokyo through the haze of Cafe Confections, where the only thing sweeter than the disserts are the girls. 

I havent yet decided where i want this story to end, the current first draft had the main character becoming a big brother archetype to the girls, while also having heavy bro-con/sis-con vibes. I'm not sure if that is the direction this should really go though.
