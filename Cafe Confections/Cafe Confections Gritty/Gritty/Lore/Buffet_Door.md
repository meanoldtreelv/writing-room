# The Buffet Door: Cafe Confections’ Darkest Secret
---
## Overview
At the far end of the hallway in Cafe Confections, past the private dining rooms, stands a **single door** with a **picture of a dessert tray** and the word **<BUFFET>**. It’s the café’s most **feared and mysterious** feature—a place only **VIPs, the manager, or "problem" hostesses** ever see.

### Key Details:
- **Location**: Far end of the hallway, **heavily guarded** by security.
- **Access**: Requires **manager approval** or a **special invitation**.
- **Rumors**: Patrons and hostesses whisper about it, but **no one speaks openly**.
- **Atmosphere**: The air near the door feels **colder**, and the usual café music **fades out**.
---
## Theories on Its Purpose
### 1. VIP Lounge
- **Elite Services**: A **private club** for high-paying clients (politicians, yakuza, celebrities).
  - **Services**: Group activities, **BDSM**, or **custom fantasies** (e.g., roleplay, humiliation).
  - **Hostesses**: Only the **most "trusted" girls** (or those being punished) are sent here.
- **Evidence**:
  - Patrons who **disappear into the door** often leave with **yakuza bodyguards**.
  - The manager **hints at "exclusive experiences"** for "loyal customers."

### 2. Snuff or Blackmail Room
- **Snuff Films**: A **hidden studio** where **illegal recordings** are made.
  - **Victims**: Hostesses who **refuse to comply** or patrons who **cross the yakuza**.
  - **Distribution**: Footage sold to **underground collectors** or used for **blackmail**.
- **Blackmail**: The café **records patrons** (via Custard’s hidden phone or cameras) and **uses the footage** to ensure silence.
- **Evidence**:
  - Custard **claims to have heard screams** from behind the door.
  - A patron once **bragged about "seeing a girl break"** but clammed up when pressed.

### 3. "Retirement" Room
- **Disposal**: Where hostesses who **outlive their usefulness** are **sent away** (or worse).
  - **Methods**: Drugged and **trafficked elsewhere**, or **killed and disposed of**.
  - **Trigger**: Attempting to escape, **stealing**, or **talking to police**.
- **Evidence**:
  - **Mint** saw a girl dragged in and **never came out**.
  - The manager **threatens girls** with the Buffet Door when they misbehave.

### 4. Yakuza Meeting Room
- **Business**: A **neutral ground** for yakuza **deals, negotiations, or punishments**.
  - **Hostesses**: Used as **bargaining chips** or **rewards** for loyal members.
  - **Patrons**: High-ranking yakuza **discuss territory, drugs, or weapons** while enjoying the café’s services.
- **Evidence**:
  - **Coco** once served drinks to **men in suits with tattoos** (classic yakuza).
  - The door is **guarded by men with earpieces** (not regular bouncers).

### 5. Initiation Chamber
- **New Hostesses**: A **test** for girls being **broken in** (e.g., gang rape, humiliation).
  - **Purpose**: Ensures they **won’t talk or run**.
  - **Survivors**: Those who **endure** become full hostesses; those who **don’t** disappear.
- **Evidence**:
  - **Custard** remembers a **"welcoming party"** when she first arrived.
  - The manager **smirks** when mentioning "training new girls."
---
## Who Controls Access?
- **The Manager**: **Only they can authorize entry** (or punishment).
- **Yakuza**: **VIP members** can request access for "special services."
- **Security**: **Two guards** stand outside; they **answer to the manager** but are **yakuza-affiliated**.

### Rules for Entry:
1. **Patrons**: Must be **pre-approved** (usually by the yakuza or manager).
2. **Hostesses**: Only sent in for **punishment, initiation, or VIP requests**.
3. **No Questions**: **Never ask** what happens inside.
---
