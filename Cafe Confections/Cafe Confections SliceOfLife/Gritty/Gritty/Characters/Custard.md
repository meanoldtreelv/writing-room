# Custard
**Role:** Hostess, "Milk Custard" Flavor
**Age:** 20
**Appearance:**
- **Thin and wiry**, with **shoulder-length dirty blonde hair**.
- **Fair skin** and **sharp, calculating eyes**.
- Wears **yellow and brown outfits** (often **loose and practical**).
---
## Personality
**Core Traits:**
- **Shy but Proactive**: **Acts demure** but **takes initiative** in services.
- **Calculating**: **Always planning**, whether it’s **blackmail or escape**.
- **Bitter**: **Hates the café** and **most of the people in it**.
- **Observant**: **Notices everything** (e.g., patron weaknesses, security blind spots).

**Contradictions:**
- **"Shy" Act**: Plays the **"gentle, submissive" role** but is **ruthless behind the scenes**.
- **Self-Preservation**: **Won’t risk herself** for others—but **might for the right price**.
- **Loyalty**: **Claims to hate everyone**, but **secretly admires Coco’s strength**.

**Lies She Tells Herself:**
- "I’m better than this place."
- "I’ll escape and never look back."
- "I don’t care what happens to the others."

**Wounds:**
- **Betrayal**: **Former idol trainee**; **blacklisted** after a **scandal with a producer**.
- **Humiliation**: **Used to be famous**; now she’s **a glorified sex worker**.
- **Paranoia**: **Convinced everyone is out to get her**—**including the other girls**.
---
## Motivations
**Primary:**
- To **gather enough blackmail** to **buy her freedom**.
- To **expose the café** and **destroy the manager**.

**Secondary:**
- To **prove she’s smarter** than everyone else.
- To **get revenge** on the producer who **ruined her career**.

**Secret:**
- **Records everything** (patrons, manager, even the other girls).
- **Plans to sell the footage** to the **yakuza or police**—whoever pays more.
---
## Relationships
**Cara:**
- **Dynamic**: **Thinks she’s fake**—but **respects her survival skills**.
- **Trust Level**: **Low**—but **would team up with her** if it **benefits her**.

**Mint:**
- **Dynamic**: **Hates her "innocent act"**—thinks it’s **a survival tactic**.
- **Trust Level**: **None**—**would throw her under the bus**.

**Coco:**
- **Dynamic**: **Mutual respect**—both are **survivors**.
- **Trust Level**: **Moderate**—**would share intel** if it **helps them both**.

**Manager:**
- **Dynamic**: **Open war**—they **suspect her of stealing** and **watch her closely**.
- **Trust Level**: **-10**—**wants to destroy them**.

**Patrons:**
- **Dynamic**: **Manipulates them** (e.g., **flirts to lower their guard**).
- **Trust Level**: **None**—**sees them as marks**.
---
## Arc
**Beginning:**
- A **jaded, scheming hostess** who **plays the game** while **planning her escape**.

**Middle:**
- **Discovers a patron’s secret** (e.g., **affair, embezzlement**) and **uses it for leverage**.
- **Clashes with the manager** when they **catch her recording**.

**End:**
- **Betrays someone** (patron, hostess, or manager) to **get her freedom**.
- **Escapes**—but **leaves chaos in her wake**.
---
## Key Scenes
- **First Meeting with David**: **Acts shy** but **studies him for weaknesses**.
- **Blackmail Setup**: **Records a patron** mid-service—**"This’ll be useful later."**
- **Confrontation with Manager**: **Caught with her phone**; **threatened with the Buffet Door**.
- **Escape**: **Sells her footage** to the yakuza and **disappears**—or **gets caught and punished**.
---
## Notes
- **Voice**: **Soft-spoken but venomous**; **sweet when manipulating**.
- **Symbolism**: **"Custard"**—**smooth on the outside, sharp and bitter inside**.
- **Growth**: **Learns to trust**—or **becomes the monster she hates**.
